A bit about me...

Hi, I'm Dean Parker. I'm a musician originally from Reading. Here's where my story began...

My Roots

I had my first guitar lesson when I was 9, and had them through my school for about a year. After getting bored, my mum encouraged me to continue and asked my uncle to teach me. He did so for 6 months but had to stop, but by this time I was improving, and good enough/enjoyed it enough to not want to stop this time.
I've been teaching myself ever since, both guitar and music theory, and have studied music for GCSE and A Level.

My Experience

I've played to live audiences of thousands, I have a bi-monthly gig at a local arts centre, and I'm a part of several local bands. I play the cajon regularly, and have dabbled in bass guitar too.

My Band

My band is a blues trio, with acoustic guitar, vocals, bass guitar and drums. We sometimes include keys, too.

Dennis Armstrong

Dennis is my bassist and often sings backing vocals. He's a musician who never ceases to amaze me - always improving, always showing me the latest skill or technique he's learnt - and I've worked with him for almost 3 years now.

Paul Youngs

Paul is the best drummer I've ever played with, and is an unnaturally natural musician. He's taught himself other instruments too, and is one of the best pianists I know.
